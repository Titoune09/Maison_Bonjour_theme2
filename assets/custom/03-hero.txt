HERO — Le Départ
================

Livrables
---------
- hero.jpg           2400 x 1600  (desktop, format paysage)
- hero-mobile.jpg    1200 x 1600  (recadrage portrait, optionnel)

Brief
-----
Image d'ouverture du site. Doit incarner "il y a une certaine France qui ne s'exporte
jamais". Plan large, atmosphère cinématographique, profondeur. Zone sombre en bas pour
lisibilité du titre blanc et du CTA. Pas de visage reconnaissable.

Prompt IA
---------
Wide cinematic photograph, early morning at a French village market in
Provence or Burgundy, cobblestone street wet from dawn rain, warm
golden light cutting through morning mist, a wooden crate of fresh
baguettes and a wicker basket in soft focus foreground, vintage Citroen
HY van blurred in background, no people visible, autumnal palette of
cream, navy, terracotta and aged brass, shot on Kodak Portra 400, 35mm
film grain, anamorphic 2:1 framing, deep shadows in lower third for
text overlay, Wes Anderson restrained color grading meets Peter Lindbergh
documentary, melancholic and inviting --ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6

Negative prompt
---------------
people faces, modern signage, cars after 1990, text, watermark, HDR,
oversharp, CGI, fantasy, tourists, plastic, neon, drone shot.
