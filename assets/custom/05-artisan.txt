ARTISAN — Atelier / Portrait
=============================

Livrables
---------
- artisan.jpg        1400 x 1750  (format portrait)
- artisan@2x.jpg     2800 x 3500

Brief
-----
Met en scène un producteur partenaire (fromager, boulanger, vigneron, confiturier).
Mains au travail privilégiées (anonymat possible). Texture, matière, geste artisanal.
Lumière naturelle. Aucune mise en scène publicitaire.

Prompt IA
---------
Close-up documentary photograph of an elderly French artisan's hands
kneading rustic sourdough dough on a flour-dusted wooden table inside
a stone-walled village bakery, deep wrinkles and flour on weathered
skin, soft directional light from a small window casting long shadows,
linen apron and rolled-up navy sleeve visible, terracotta tiled floor
out of focus, single warm tungsten bulb glowing in background, Kodak
Portra 400 film grain, ivory cream and earth tone palette, Sebastiao
Salgado meets editorial food photography, dignified and quiet
--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6

Negative prompt
---------------
young hands, gloves, factory, stainless steel, plastic, logo, text,
hyperreal, CGI, smiling face, posed, oversharp, HDR.

Variations
----------
A) Mains pétrissant pâte boulanger
B) Fromager découpant meule à la lyre, cave d'affinage
C) Vigneron taillant vigne en hiver, brume
D) Confiturière remuant cuivre à la louche bois
