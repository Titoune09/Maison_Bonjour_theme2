CURRENT BOX — Box du mois
==========================

Livrables
---------
- current-box.jpg     1600 x 1600   (format carré)
- current-box@2x      3200 x 3200
- variations mensuelles à prévoir (1 photo / mois)

Brief
-----
Image produit éditoriale de la box ouverte. Doit donner envie de soulever le couvercle.
Composition étudiée mais naturelle. Fond neutre crème ou bois patiné. Lumière diffuse
type lightbox naturelle. Tous les produits visibles, lisibles, étiquettes lisibles.

Prompt IA
---------
Overhead editorial product photograph of an open vintage cardboard
subscription box with kraft tissue paper revealing artisanal French
groceries: a small jar of apricot jam, a wedge of comte cheese
wrapped in wax paper, a bottle of olive oil, a packet of fleur de sel,
a tin of sardines with illustrated label, a baguette diagonally placed,
dried lavender sprig, handwritten card with wax seal, arranged on a
weathered ivory linen cloth, soft north-facing window light, gentle
shadows, Kodak Portra 400 film aesthetic, cream and navy and terracotta
palette, top-down 90 degree angle, generous breathing room around
composition, Cereal magazine style --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6

Negative prompt
---------------
plastic packaging, modern barcode, supermarket brand, neon, CGI render,
oversaturated, harsh flash, cluttered, watermark, text overlay, hands
in frame.
